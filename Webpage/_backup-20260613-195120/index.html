The Exam at a Glance

Know what you're walking into

Format

Up to 90 questions in 90 minutes — multiple-choice plus performance-based questions (PBQs), which usually appear first.

Passing Score

750 on a scale of 100–900. PBQs are time sinks — flag them, finish the multiple choice, then come back.

Strategy

Most questions hinge on picking the BEST answer among several plausible ones. Read for qualifiers: first, best, most, least.
